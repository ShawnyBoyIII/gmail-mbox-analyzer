## 2024-06-06 - Explicit Actions vs Auto-Selection
**Learning:** Depending solely on auto-selection and keyboard shortcuts (like Ctrl+C) for copying critical text isn't intuitive for all users, particularly non-power users.
**Action:** Always provide explicit action buttons like "Copy to Clipboard" with temporary visual feedback (e.g., "Copied! ✓") to improve clarity and usability for text intended to be exported or copied from the UI.
