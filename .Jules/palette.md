## 2024-06-08 - Explicit Copy to Clipboard Actions
**Learning:** Automatically selecting text in a read-only input and expecting users to use keyboard shortcuts (Cmd/Ctrl+C) is a poor accessibility and UX pattern. Many users may not know the shortcut or may struggle to use it, especially in popups or modals.
**Action:** Always provide an explicit "Copy to Clipboard" button paired with clear visual feedback ("Copied! ✓") when presenting users with generated strings or tokens they are expected to copy.
